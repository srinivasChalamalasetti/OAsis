Sundar Pichai

CEO of Alphabet Inc.

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive born on July 12, 1972, in Chennai, India. He pursued his education at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, where he earned a Bachelor's degree in Metallurgical Engineering.

Afterward, he received a scholarship to Stanford University, where he earned a Master's degree in Material Sciences and Engineering. He further earned an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Pichai joined Google in 2004, initially working on the Google Toolbar and then leading the development of Google Chrome. His leadership led to the creation of the Chrome browser and later the Chrome OS.

He also played a key role in the development of Google Drive and the Android operating system. His exceptional skills and leadership qualities earned him the role of CEO of Google in 2015.

Under his leadership, Google continued to thrive and expand its influence across various industries. In 2019, he also became the CEO of Alphabet Inc., Google's parent company.

Sundar Pichai is known for his visionary approach to technology and his commitment to making information accessible to all. He continues to inspire countless individuals around the world.

His journey from a small town in India to becoming a global tech leader is a testament to his dedication, intelligence, and unwavering work ethic.
